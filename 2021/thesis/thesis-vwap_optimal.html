# 实证研究

303030 different stocks which make up the Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJIA), on 606060 days 从TAQ获取, We processe the raw data to obtain daily series of market volumes (正整数) and average period price (有理数), 每一个interval都是1分钟

过滤带有以下条件的交易记录 (ignore market opening/closing and over-the-counter trade[1])

  • correction code greater than 1, trade data incorrect;
  • sales condition "4", "@4", "C4", "N4", "R4", derivatively priced, i.e., the trade was executed over-the-counter (or in an external facility like a Dark Pool)
  • sales condition "T" or "U", extended hours trades (before or after the official market hours);
  • sales condition "V", stock option trades (which are also executed over-the-counter);
  • sales condition "Q", "O", "M", "6", opening trades and closing trades (the opening and closing auctions).

We found few works in the literature concerned with intraday market volumes modeling (假设有kkk只股票, 每一个时间段Δt=1\Delta t = 1Δt=1 min)

f(m1(k),m2(k),⋯,mT(k))∼ln⁡N(μ+1bk,Σ)f\left(m_1^{(k)}, m_2^{(k)},\cdots, m_T^{(k)}\right) \sim \ln\mathcal{N}(\mu+\mathbf{1}b^{k}, \Sigma) f(m1(k)​,m2(k)​,⋯,mT(k)​)∼lnN(μ+1bk,Σ)

T=390T = 390T=390 intervals, W = 202020 days, K=30K=30K=30 stocks (所以每天都有一个新的估计, 从第20天开始)

任务量是1%1 \%1%日均交易量, 他们假设这样的交易量不足以产生价格影响, 成交价格只受价差/流动性的短暂影响

p^t=pt(1−0.5st+0.5αstut/mt)\hat p_t = p_t \left(1-0.5s_t + 0.5\alpha s_t u_t / m_t \right) p^​t​=pt​(1−0.5st​+0.5αst​ut​/mt​)

我们的优化目标是transaction cost slippage, 就是痛VWAP交易成本的比较, 越小越好, QQQ是起始任务量(==凭什么假定Q都可以以VWAP成交?==)
