非线性市场影响

Xiang Liu 2021-01-25 ThesisReview

这篇文章主要参考同年发表在Quant. Finance上Optimal execution with non-linear transient market impact的一篇研究

# 摘要

要优化交易策略, 首先得有一个市场影响模型, 过去研究假定transient impact是线性的, 这篇研究通过假设一个非线性模型, 发现这样会产生price manipulation和negative expected cost的问题, 通过引入买卖价差或者impose convexity of the transient impact function for large trading rate来避免这个问题,

# 介绍

informed traders prefer to trade incrementally through time (Kyle, 1985). This strategy allows the trader to minimize costs while also minimizing the revelation of information to the rest of the market.

two impact components: temporary one that only affects the individual trade and the permanent one that affects both current and future trades equally.

focus: more on the transient nature of market impact

# 结论

为了避免白嫖, 要么引入wide spread, 要么引入penalty或者让影响函数尽量不那么凹

a high spread cost acts as a strong disincentive to wrong way trading
