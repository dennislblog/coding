# Volume Weighted Average Price Optimal Execution

  1. Minimize mean-variance of the slippage, with quadratic transaction costs

  2. Most literatures assume perfect knowledge of the total market volume

  3. Their solution reduces by 10% the VWAP deviation RMSE of the standard static solution
